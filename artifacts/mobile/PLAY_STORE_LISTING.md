## Full Description (max 4,000 characters)

Deen o Dunya Planner is a complete Islamic companion designed to help Muslims organize their spiritual and daily life in one place.

**Features:**
- **Accurate Prayer Times** — automatic location-based prayer times from the AlAdhan API, with Hijri date display.
- **Adhan Notifications** — optional Adhan alarm at every prayer time, even when the app is closed.
- **Qibla Compass** — find the direction to the Kaaba with a smooth, anti-jitter compass.
- **Quran Reader** — read the Holy Quran, switch translations, and browse tafsir in multiple languages.
- **Keyword Search** — quickly find verses by topic or category.
- **Masjid Finder** — discover nearby mosques using OpenStreetMap and open directions in your maps app.
- **Tasbeeh Counter** — dhikr counter with common recitations.
- **Zakat Calculator** — calculate your annual Zakat based on gold, silver, cash, and debts.
- **Daily Hadith** — a hand-picked hadith for every day of the year.
- **Prayer Tracker** — mark your daily prayers as completed and build consistency.
- **Planner & Tasks** — organize your Islamic daily goals and to-do lists.
- **99 Names of Allah** — learn and reflect on the beautiful names of Allah.
- **Islamic Calendar & Seerah** — essential dates and stories from the life of the Prophet ﷺ.

Available in **English**, **Arabic**, and **Urdu**. RTL support included for Arabic and Urdu.

Deen o Dunya Planner — helping you balance your Deen and Dunya, one day at a time.
